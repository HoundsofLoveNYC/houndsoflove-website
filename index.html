About me

Hi! My name is Mendé and I’ve been a full-time petsitter and dog walker for three years, caring for hundreds of different animals through another company and have now ventured out on my own!
I value the opportunity petsitting gives me: to care for and share my love with animals in my free time. Working on my own, rather than for a large company with a cast of rotating walkers, I treasure the chance to build trusting, reliable relationships with my animals and their families!
If you’re interested in working with me, please send me a text or email!
