About me

Hi! My name is Mendé and I’ve been a full-time petsitter and dog walker since 2015, caring for hundreds of different animals through other companies and now am working on my own! I value the opportunity petsitting gives me: to care for and share my love with animals and help the people who love them that need someone to count on.

I've been petsitting full time for 4 years and recently transitioned to working on my own after being "on the apps" in combination with my regular clients! I started petsitting on the side to bring joy into my life and to help out owners and their animals in my spare time, and it just took over my life!

I operate solo and have experience with dogs and cats (and birds!) of all energy types, age, and need. I have worked with elderly dogs with mobility issues, dog with separation anxiety, administered medication (pills and injections), and have done wound care for animals fresh out of surgery. I regularly pick animals up from daycare when their caregivers will be home late and often take my regulars to vet appointments for an hourly rate.

Working on my own, rather than for a large company with a cast of rotating walkers, I treasure the chance to build trusting, reliable relationships with my animals and their families! I only work with one client at a time. I do not do pack walks unless the clients have asked, specifically, to walk with a neighbor in the same building.

My flexible schedule allows me to accommodate most last-minute requests, as well as affords me the opportunity to volunteer my labor in my free time with a local harm reduction organization.

I also donate $50 from my profits each month to an organization that works with criminalized survivors. I offer a one time 5% discount if you, too, contribute to their work. Ask me for more information!

I'd love to schedule a meet and greet soon and talk about how I can meet your needs. If you’re interested in working together, please send me a text or email!
