Hounds of Love Pet Care

Pet care services in Brooklyn and Lower Manhattan

About me

Hi! My name is Mendé and I’ve been a full-time petsitter and dog walker for three years, caring for hundreds of different animals through another company and have now ventured out on my own! I value the opportunity petsitting gives me: to care for and share my love with animals in my free time. Working on my own, rather than for a large company with a cast of rotating walkers, I treasure the chance to build trusting, reliable relationships with my animals and their families! If you’re interested in working with me, please send me a text[link] or email[link]!

Pricing

$22 30-minute walk (scheduled)
$29 30-minute walk (day-of requests)
$29 30-minute drop-in visits
$35 8-hr daycare (in your home)
$69 24-hr overnight housesitting ($31 for additional pet)
